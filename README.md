# 🛒 Brazilian E-Commerce Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the Brazilian E-commerce Public Dataset by Olist using PostgreSQL and Excel.

The goal of this analysis is to identify:

-Revenue Trends
-Sales Growth Patterns
-Top-performing Product Categories
-Customer Distribution
-Delivery Performance
-Customer Satisfaction Insights 

The project focuses on transforming raw transactional data into business insights through SQL anlysis and dashboard visualization.

# 📊 Key Business Questions

This project answers the following business questions:

1. How is the company’s revenue changing over time?
2. What are the monthly sales growth trends?
3. Which products categories generate the highest revenue?
4. Which regions experience the slowest deliveries?
5. Does delivery performance affect customer review scores?
4. Which regions contain the highest customer concentration?

# 🔍 Key Insights

## 📈 Revenue Growth
Revenue steadily increased throughout 2017 and peaked during late 2017 to early 2018.

## 📈 Monthly Sales Growth

Sales growth fluctuated throughout the analysis period, with rapid expansion during 2017 followed by more stable growth patterns in 2018.

## 🛍 Top Product Categories
Health Beauty and Watches Gifts generated the highest revenue among all product categories.

## ⭐ Customer Satisfaction
Orders delivered on time received significantly higher review scores compared to delayed deliveries.

## 🚚 Delivery Performance
Northern regions experienced longer average delivery times compared to southeastern regions.

## 🌍 Customer Distribution
São Paulo had the highest customer concentration, significantly outperforming other regions.
